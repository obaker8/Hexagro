HEXAGRO

Founded in London, Ontario and located in Iqaluit, Nunavut, HexaGro is a youth-led startup social enterprise that strives to become the forefront of farming technology by specializing in the production of affordable and energy efficient gardens, called the HexaGro Greenhouse.

ABOUT HEXAGRO OUR TEAM

OUR GOAL

With HexaGro Greenhouses, our company aims to solve the widespread problem of food insecurity in Northern Canada, as the latter presents a serious and growing challenge. In 2011, off reserve households in Canada were about twice as likely as other Canadian households to be food insecure, due to inaccessibility, unaffordability and malnutrition.

HEXAGRO GREENHOUSE

HexaGro is a compact, small-scale and easy to use greenhouse in the form of a hexagonal prism which provides Northern Canadians the opportunity to grow their own food by providing them with the appropriate environmental conditions. The simplistic design will not only allow for crop growth and harvest for majority of the year, but will save the consumer a significant expense for fresh foods.
