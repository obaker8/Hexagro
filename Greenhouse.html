THE HEXAGRO GREENHOUSE

Scroll down to see more about our design and prices.

The HexaGro Greenhouse—the first product released by Hexagro—is a small-scale and primarily mechanical greenhouse in the form of a hexagonal prism. Providing an affordable, efficient and effective food source, our product gives Northern Canadians the opportunity to grow their own produce by providing the precise environmental conditions for the plants to thrive. One of the major focuses of our product is to provide a better alternative solution to the skyrocketing cost of food in Northern Canada. HexaGro Greenhouses are self-sufficient, energy-efficient, effective, as well as affordable.


HexaGro Greenhouses are composed of light, thin, high-strength resistant polycarbonate sheets, which increase the greenhouse effect. As our brand focuses on developing our product to be easy to use and customer friendly, the application of the greenhouse is simple and straightforward. The package comes with five components - soil, base, cover, fans and panels. The customers will be provided a “HexaGro Greenhouse User Guide”, an extensive step-by-step tutorial with visuals to aid in greenhouse set up.  


Each greenhouse has a diameter of 2 metres and a height of 1.4 metres. The greenhouse is used for eight months of the year, April to November. The life of the greenhouses are approximately eight to ten years.


The greenhouse in a shape of a hexagon to maximize the surface area and incorporate biomimicry. The internal temperature of the greenhouse is controlled through thermal mass, which retains heat and releases when the temperatures drops under 15℃. Through the mechanical thermostats attached to the inside wall of the greenhouse, verifying the temperature becomes possible. If temperature rises, opening up the door will fix the problem; as well as if the temperature drops, horticultural fleeces will keep the plants in the environmental conditions to help them thrive.


An irrigation system eases the process of watering plants. As there are six different compartments, there can be maximum of six different plants in one greenhouse. Our brand understands that keeping up with six different watering intervals for each plants can be quite difficult. The function of our irrigation system is to help our customers be organized. By having a 6cm tall tank on top of the cover, plants can be watered separately. The water will be collected by 10 cm tank attached to the bottom of the greenhouse. An option as an add on to our HexaGro Greenhouse are the solar panels. The solar panels will be contribute to maintaining the precise environment needed for the plants to thrive.
